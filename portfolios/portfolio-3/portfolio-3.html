Analysis/Reflection

I’ve been familiar with number systems for a long time due to my exposure to technology. I often encounter hexadecimal codes when selecting color patterns for projects, and binary (1’s and 0’s) when dealing with logic gates, funnily, in Minecraft when I was addicted to the game during my younger days. And, of course, the decimal system is the most common, used in daily life, which all of us are aware of. The only system I had little knowledge of was octal, but my curiosity about how computers work gave me a rough idea of it as well. Upon studying these systems more deeply, I realized they are much more prevalent than I initially thought.

Analysis/Reflection

For us students, understanding these technologies will allow us to contribute effectively to our future jobs, and being familiar with these tools can open up new opportunities for innovation and problem-solving in various fields even outside the realm of Computer Science and Information Technology. Understanding these concepts as Computer Science students will equip us to succeed in a tech-driven world, not only because it is part of our careers but also because we may find it in unexpected areas such as business organizations. I find that learning about these systems as students will be very useful in the future. Like mentioned, this knowledge will come in very handy for those of us who will work in organizations and more especially for those of us who strive to become tech entrepreneurs.

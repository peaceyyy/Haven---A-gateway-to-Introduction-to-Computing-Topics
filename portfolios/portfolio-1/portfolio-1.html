Computer Science as a Discipline ○ Portfolio #1

Analysis/Reflection

Computer Science and its various disciplines have always been confusing for me, especially when it came to choosing my course. It's crucial for me to understand the different fields and subfields of computer science to align them with my interests and career goals. The overlaps between Computer Science, Information Technology, and Information Systems highlight the importance of recognizing their subtle similarities and differences in my life and career. I find it fascinating how diverse the field is while still centering around computers and technology. I hope to explore and immerse myself in these interdisciplinary areas of computing.
